About the webpage author

Gian Marco de la Cruz is a student at Philippine Science High School - Southern Mindanao Campus. He likes coding, listening to music, and playing video games. He created westphilsea.com as a school project for Computer Science 3 and to share his findings on the ongoing situation about the West Philippine Sea.
