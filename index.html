As tensions rise between the Philippines and China in their fight over the West Philippine Sea, people must learn more about this problem in order to be informed and help solve the problem. This website aims to help you:
  • Learn more about the West Philippine Sea and the areas within its boundaries,
  • Learn about the conflict between the Philippines and China over the ownership of the West Philippine Sea,
  • Learn about both countries' histories with the area
